About

Hi, I’m Devorah - A software developer with a creative eye and a knack for technical challenges. I recently completed my bachelor's degree in Computer and Information Science and a Certificate in Full Stack Web Development from Actualize and am in my final semesters of a Bachelor of Science in Computer and Informational Sciences at Hebrew Theological Institute.

My favorite part about writing code is exercising my creative muscle and learning to communicate with my computer, and I get to do that each day as I learn and practice these skills. When I’m not at my computer or hitting the textbooks, I’m reading, writing fantasy, or hiking in the mountains. I never pass up an opportunity to talk history or Star Wars, and I’m passionate about all things fantasy.

Let’s connect! I’d love to collaborate, learn something new, or discuss our mutual of creative writing. Drop me a message here

Work

Ralla Klepak Foundation - PAL Initiative

In January of 2023, I was hired by the Ralla Klepak foundation to work as their database developer. Since the inception of the program three years ago most of the data about participatns and budgets was done on paper. My first and most time consuming task was to digitize the paper records. Shortly after starting at this position I was also given the task of updating and managing the PAL Initiative website.

Education

In the summer of 2022 I graduated from Touro College: Hebrew Theological Institute where I recieved a bachelor's degree in Computer and Information Sciences.

As part of my degree I participated in the Actualize coding bootcamp, where I learned skills in coding with Ruby and JavaScript and recieved a Certificate in Full Stack Web Developer.

Bootcamp

- Learned fundamentals and best practices in full-stack web development in areas of project architecture, backend, frontend, and how to effectively self-teach new technologies

- Generated technical requirements and determined the most effective solutions, created wireframes, designed schema, and researched outside resources

- Built and tested the backend, incorporating external APIs

- Created pages and RESTful routes, made web requests to retrieve data, formatted and styled results, incorporated libraries and themes

Projects

Capstone Project - Promptly

A web app that allows users to generate and view writing prompts for authors with writer’s block. The app is built utilizing Ruby on Rails and Vue.js as frameworks, and Ruby, JS and HTML languages to create a database of ideas. To take a look at the website, click here!

Watch the website presentation


Honors Program Project - BRAINstorm

A journaling website and database built for a college Honors Program's final project. Brainstorm can be used to create notes and store them securely under an account and also lists prompts to help unstick a writer's brain.
